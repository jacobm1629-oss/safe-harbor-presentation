🇺🇸 Federal IRC Compliance

Under ERISA and the Internal Revenue Code, employee benefit taxation is governed exclusively at the federal level across all 50 states.

  • ✔ IRS Section 125: Allows valid pre-tax deductions for licensed health platforms.
  • ✔ IRS Section 105(b): Authorizes tax-free reimbursements for medical expenses.
  • ✔ Supreme Law: Local state authorization is not required to launch or benefit from these programs today.
